# 🎯 Business Requirements

This document outlines the core business requirements and analytical objectives that the Google Play Store Analytics project addresses.

## Analytical Objectives

1. **Market Share Analysis**: Identify which app categories dominate the Google Play Store in terms of application count and install volume.
2. **Pricing Optimization**: Assess the distribution of free vs. paid apps and identify typical pricing sweet spots across different categories.
3. **Quality & Feedback Loop**: Study the distribution of user ratings and reviews to understand overall quality benchmarks.
4. **Sentiment Analytics**: Analyze user reviews using sentiment analysis to extract common trends and customer satisfaction indicators.
5. **Revenue Optimization**: Identify high-revenue generating categories and pricing categories to maximize return on investment.
6. **Technical Benchmarking**: Understand the relationship between application size, target Android versions, update frequencies, and how they impact user ratings.
